Gabby Boyd

Theater Arts Freshman at Bob Jones University

College Life

I am a freshman at Bob Jones University studying theater arts with a minor in creative writing. I work for the Mack Library at the circulation desk. I enjoy reading and writing books and hope to publish some one day. English and grammar have always been one of my favorite subjects, and I am frequently asked to read over other people's essays for classes. The library is a nice place to relax and research for projects or just for enjoyment.

Ambitions

I volunteer to shelve at my local bookstore in Corning, New York. I am actively involved in my home church with Vacation Bible School, Sunday school, and I hope to find a local church here in Greenville to participate in. I really like working with children, which is what I hope my theater major will help me expand on. I want to work as an elementary/high school play director and/or volunteer directing plays at church. 

Unique Facts

I really love dill pickles and have chugged an entire jar of both the pickles and the juice in one sitting. I grew up on a farm and once taught my entire algebra class the fertilization process because one girl refused to eat "unborn chicks" (eggs). I have a fiancé; we had been dating for over a year, and we are planning to get married next summer.
